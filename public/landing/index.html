Memento

Simple Online Diary

  • Create a free account

Daily Email Reminders

Optionable feature. Subscribe in Settings, if you want to acquire the habit of regular journaling. By default we send out email reminders at 6pm UTC.

Attach Pictures

Turn your diary in a beautiful calendar with ability to attach a picture to every entry. Images and photos will help you to keep your memories fresh and vivid.

Detailed Statistics

Keep track of your journaling via Stats Page. Here you can find information about your writing in figures, estimate regularity, and find out what is important for you with help of 'Most Frequent Words'.

Simple Search

Find specific days of yours with ability to search through all of your entries. Search for names of your relatives to discover precious memories about time spent with them.
